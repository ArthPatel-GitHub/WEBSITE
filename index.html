What are Esports?

Esports, short for electronic sports, is a form of competition using video games. It often takes the form of organized, multiplayer video game competitions, particularly between professional players, individually or as teams. The esports industry has grown significantly in recent years, with large audiences, professional leagues, and substantial prize pools.

Sentinels

Sentinels is a renowned professional esports and entertainment organization based in the United States. They are known for their successful teams in various games, including Valorant, Fortnite, and Halo.

LOUD

LOUD is a leading esports organization based in Brazil. They have gained international recognition for their teams' performances in games like League of Legends, Free Fire, and Valorant.

NRG

NRG Esports is an American esports organization established in 2015. They have teams competing in several popular games such as Overwatch, Valorant, Apex Legends, and Rocket League.
