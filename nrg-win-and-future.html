Wins & Future

Our Wins

We are the best. We have been been a top contender in esports, with multiple championship titles under our belt. Here are some of our most notable victories:

  • Overwatch League Champions - 2019
  • Fortnite World Cup Winners - 2020
  • Valorant International Tournament - 2022
  • Rocket League Championship Series - 2023

We still plan on to get stronger by the second, dominating in all aspects of every competition and championships. Only striving to win more.

The Future of NRG

NRG is always looking forward. As e-sports continues to grow, we aim to expand into new games, engage with more fans, and continue setting trends in the industry.

We have many future goals. We are expanding our team to new games like Apex Legends and League of Legends so we can dominate in all games, we also are going to be building a global fan base with new partnerships. We are making innovating ideas with our training programs and technologies. Most importantly, Setting new records in e-sports viewership and participation like never before.
