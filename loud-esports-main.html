About Loud

Loud is a Brazilian esports organization known for their successful teams in games like Free Fire, League of Legends, and Valorant. They have a significant presence in the Latin American esports scene and are famous for their competitive spirit and community engagement.

History

Loud was founded in 2019 and quickly rose to prominence in the esports world. Their dynamic approach and engaging content have earned them millions of followers, making them one of the most celebrated teams in Brazil.

Major Achievements

  • 2021 Free Fire World Series Champions
  • 2020 CBLOL (Campionato Brasileiro de League of Legends) Champions
  • 2021 Valorant Champions Tour Brazil Masters Winners

Current Roster

  • Free Fire Team: Will, ViniZx, Two9, Brabox, and DGG
  • League of Legends Team: Robo, Tay, CarioK, Dynquedo, Ceos
  • Valorant Team: Sacy, Pancada, Saadhak, Less, Aspas
