What are esports

Esports, short for electronic sports, is a form of competition using video games. It often takes the form of organized, multiplayer video game competitions, particularly between professional players, individually or as teams.

Sentinels

Sentinels is a professional esports and entertainment organization based in the United States. They are known for their teams in various games like Valorant, Fortnite, and Halo.

Loud

Loud is a Brazilian esports organization known for their successful teams in games like Free Fire, League of Legends, and Valorant. They are a significant presence in the Latin American esports scene.

NRG Esports

NRG Esports is an American esports organization with teams in games such as Rocket League, Overwatch, and Apex Legends. They are known for their competitive edge and dynamic player rosters.
