What are esports

Esports, short for electronic sports, is a form of competition using video games. It often takes the form of organized, multiplayer video game competitions, particularly between professional players, individually or as teams.

Sentinels

Sentinels is a professional esports and entertainment organization based in the United States. They are known for their teams in various games like Valorant, Fortnite, and Halo.

G2 Esports

G2 Esports is a European esports organization based in Berlin, Germany.
